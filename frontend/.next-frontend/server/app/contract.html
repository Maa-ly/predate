Reactive Sentinel

The Sentinel runs on Lasna (Reactive Network) and watches Sepolia events, then triggers manager rebalances via callback transactions.

What the numbers mean
When the Sentinel executes reactive subscriptions/callbacks, it can accrue debt tracked by the Lasna system contract. Keep the Sentinel funded with REACT, then call `coverDebt()` to pay the system contract from the Sentinel balance.
